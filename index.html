Better Eggs for Better Nutrition

Eggs are one of the most nutrient-dense foods available and are rich in vitamins and minerals. They are also a good source of high-quality protein. Suguna Foods introduced for the first time in India a range of premium quality Suguna Value Added Eggs, now Delfrez Eggs, enriched with special nutrients such as Omega 3 fatty acids, Selenium, Vitamins, and minerals that not only provide nutritional value but specific functional benefits to the consumer. The four different types of eggs are each fortified with unique vitamins and nutrients to enable individuals of all ages and health problems to maintain a balanced diet and healthy lifestyle. Delfrez Eggs come in 6-egg container boxes as a ‘value for money’ product and are available at leading supermarkets and Delfrez outlets across the country.

The four varieties of Delfrez Eggs are: Active, Heart, Sharp, Energy, Gold, Fit

Active

Delfrez Active is specifically created for children, adults, pregnant women, lactating mothers, and individuals who have an active lifestyle and require extra nourishment. These are farm fresh eggs with Docosahexaenoic acid (DHA), Organic Selenium, and Vitamin E. The enrichment is accomplished by providing special natural feed to the hens raised on Suguna’s bio security certified farms. Delfrez Active aids in the improvement of memory, visual development, physical performance, and emotional stability. The DHA in Active is necessary for prenatal brain and vision development. Organic Selenium is crucial for thyroid function, which helps to minimize the risk of major diseases such as cancer, whereas Vitamin E is an antioxidant that protects skin and cells from UV radiation and free radicals. It has been shown to reduce fine lines and wrinkles while also improving general health.

Heart

Delfrez Heart eggs are intended for health-conscious individuals who desire a low-cholesterol egg with the advantages of Omega 3 and Vitamin E for longevity and a healthy heart. Delfrez Heart is a high-value farm fresh egg with up to 24% reduced cholesterol. It is rich in Fatty acids, low in cholesterol, and high in Vitamin E, a natural antioxidant. Omega–3 fatty acids are a kind of polyunsaturated fat that is helpful to health, but since it is not generated by the human body, it must be obtained from diet. Omega–3 fatty acids help lower blood cholesterol levels, decreasing the risk of clogged arteries. Delfrez Heart, with its reduced cholesterol, aids in the maintenance of a healthy heart.

Sharp

Delfrez Sharp is a farm fresh egg with additional value that is high in protein and vitamins. The enrichment is accomplished by providing special natural feed to the hens kept on Suguna’s bio-security certified farms. The high protein in Delfrez Pro helps build and repair the cells in muscles and other body tissues. It is also rich in Vitamin A, which promotes healthy skin, bones, and eyes. Furthermore, Lutein and Zeaxanthin are two significant pigments in Delfrez Pro that aid in the prevention of macular degeneration and cataracts of the eyes. Calcium and phosphorus also aid in the creation of bones and teeth. We follow stringent standards to guarantee that the eggs are tested, disinfected, and stamped with a quality seal before being packaged in pulp cardboard cartons. Suguna Pro eggs include extra vitamins and proteins for a healthy lifestyle.

Energy

Delfrez’s Energy is suitable for people of all ages, as well as those who lead an active lifestyle and require additional nutrients. Energy is a collection of fat-soluble vitamins that are crucial for enhancing intestinal calcium absorption, which supports the formation and maintenance of healthy bones and teeth in people of all ages. People who avoid the sun or use sunscreen on their skin must incorporate a good amount of Vitamin D in their diet. Adequate levels of Vitamin D intake reduces the risk of Osteomalacia (Softening of bones), Rickets (Bone deformity), Heart diseases, Cancer, Hypertension and Type 2 diabetes (in later ages). Vitamin D also plays a very important role in enhancing the immunity.
